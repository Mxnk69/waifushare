This is a list about the Husbandos in anime.

1.Gojo Saturo


Satoru Gojo is a prominent character in the manga and anime series Jujutsu Kaisen, created by Gege Akutami. He is one of the most powerful sorcerers in the series and serves as a teacher at Jujutsu High.
Gojo comes from the prestigious Gojo family, known for their powerful jujutsu sorcerers. He is a direct descendant of the powerful Six Eyes and Limitless techniques.
Gojo possesses the Limitless technique, which allows him to manipulate space at an atomic level. This grants him incredible abilities, such as creating an impenetrable barrier and controlling the flow of time within certain bounds.
Gojo is known for his charismatic and laid-back personality. He often displays a playful attitude, even in serious situations, which contrasts with the dark themes of the series.
Gojo's character has garnered a large fanbase due to his intriguing mix of humor, depth, and overwhelming power. He serves as a central figure in the unfolding events of Jujutsu Kaisen, significantly influencing the series' narrative and the development of other characters. His dynamic presence adds both levity and intensity to the story, making him a key element in its popularity.

2.Nanami

Kento Nanami is a key character in Jujutsu Kaisen, known for his serious demeanor and exceptional skills as a jujutsu sorcerer.
Nanami previously worked as a salaryman before returning to the world of jujutsu sorcery. His experience in the corporate world contrasts sharply with his role as a sorcerer, highlighting the duality of his character.
Nanami possesses a unique jujutsu technique called the Ratio Technique, which allows him to divide his opponent’s body into sections and deal critical damage. He can create a "10:0" ratio, effectively enhancing his attacks.
Nanami is characterized by his no-nonsense attitude. He approaches his duties with professionalism, often emphasizing the importance of hard work and responsibility.
Nanami grapples with the meaning of his work as a sorcerer, often reflecting on the burdens and moral complexities of fighting curses. His character explores themes of purpose, sacrifice, and the weight of responsibility.
Nanami's character is significant for his depth and relatability. He represents the struggles faced by those in high-pressure jobs, whether in the corporate world or in jujutsu sorcery. His interactions with Yuji and other characters highlight the importance of mentorship and the moral dilemmas inherent in their fight against curses. His story arc contributes to the overall themes of the series, making him a fan-favorite character.

3.Loid

Loid Forger is the main character of the popular manga and anime series Spy x Family, created by Tatsuya Endo. He is a highly skilled spy known by the codename "Twilight."
Loid works as a secret agent for a country called Ostania. His missions often involve espionage and gathering intelligence, reflecting his status as one of the best spies in his organization.
Loid is characterized by his calm demeanor and quick thinking. He often remains collected under pressure, making him a proficient spy.
Loid adopts Anya, a young girl with telepathic abilities. Their relationship is central to the story, as Anya often knows more about Loid’s mission than he realizes. Their bond grows, highlighting themes of love and connection.
Loid's character explores themes of identity, deception, and the complexities of family. His role as a spy requires him to constantly wear a mask, which reflects the challenges of forming genuine relationships.
Loid Forger’s character resonates with fans due to his multifaceted personality and the heartwarming dynamics within the Forger family. His journey illustrates the challenges of trust and the importance of connections, making Spy x Family a blend of action, comedy, and heartfelt moments.

4. Sukuna

Ryomen Sukuna, often simply referred to as Sukuna, is a major antagonist in Jujutsu Kaisen, created by Gege Akutami. He is a powerful curse and serves as a central figure in the series’ lore and conflict.
Sukuna was once a human sorcerer who lived over a thousand years ago. Known for his immense power and cruelty, he became infamous for his strength and sadistic nature. After his death, he transformed into a curse, embodying the negative emotions and fear associated with his legacy.
Sukuna possesses formidable cursed energy and has a wide array of powerful techniques, including the ability to manipulate cursed energy at will. His strength makes him a lethal opponent.
Sukuna is bound to Yuji Itadori after Yuji consumes one of his fingers, which contain part of Sukuna's cursed energy. This bond creates a unique dynamic between the two, as Sukuna often interacts with Yuji, sometimes offering guidance but primarily seeking to manipulate him.
ukuna embodies the chaotic and destructive aspects of curses, serving as a foil to Yuji’s more compassionate nature. Their interactions highlight the themes of morality, identity, and the nature of good and evil.
Sukuna's character is essential to the plot of Jujutsu Kaisen, providing tension and conflict as he poses a significant threat to both the protagonists and the world. His complexity and the philosophical questions he raises contribute to the series’ depth, making him a memorable and intriguing antagonist.

5.Kurumi

Kurumi Tokisaki is one of the main characters in the Date A Live series.
She is known as the "Nightmare" and is one of the most powerful Spirits.
Kurumi is distinguished by her gothic lolita outfit and her ability to manipulate time using her powers.
She has a complex personality, often oscillating between being a ruthless antagonist and a tragic figure with a hidden agenda.

6.Ellen Joe

Ellen Joe is a character from the action role-playing game Zenless Zone Zero, developed by HoYoverse.
In the game, Ellen Joe is depicted as a character with distinctive traits and background that contribute to the game's storyline.
Zenless Zone Zero is set in a post-apocalyptic world where players navigate through various challenges and interact with a diverse cast of characters.
Ellen Joe's role and characteristics are part of what makes the game's narrative engaging and immersive.

7.Marin Kitagawa

Marin Kitagawa is a fictional character from the popular manga and anime series My Dress-Up Darling (also known as Sono Bisque Doll wa Koi wo Suru), created by Shinichi Fukuda. The series first began serialization in 2018.
Marin is a high school student known for her outgoing personality and passion for cosplay. She is a major character in the series and becomes the central focus of the story's plot.
Marin has long, blonde hair and striking green eyes. Her appearance is often noted for its vibrancy and attention to detail, which reflects her enthusiasm for cosplay.
Marin is lively, confident, and unapologetically enthusiastic about her interests. She has a warm and approachable demeanor, making her well-liked among her peers.
The story revolves around her collaboration with a classmate, Wakana Gojou, who is a skilled craftsman specializing in hina dolls. Marin asks Wakana to help her create elaborate costumes for her cosplay, and the series explores their evolving friendship and romantic undertones.
Marin's character development is central to the series, as it delves into her personal growth, her love for cosplay, and her interactions with Wakana. The series highlights themes of self-expression, creativity, and the bond between the characters.

8.Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa Ackerman is a prominent character from the manga and anime series Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin), created by Hajime Isayama.
Mikasa has long, black hair that she often wears in a red scarf, a symbol of her past and connection to her family.
She has grayish-blue eyes.
In her military uniform, she typically wears the standard Survey Corps attire, including a brown jacket with the emblem of the corps, green cloak, and the vertical maneuvering equipment.
Mikasa plays a crucial role in the fight against the Titans and the various conflicts that arise throughout the series. Her loyalty, strength, and strategic mind make her an invaluable member of the Survey Corps.
Mikasa is fiercely protective of those she cares about, particularly her adoptive brother, Eren Yeager. She is known for her stoic and serious demeanor but shows deep emotional depth and a strong sense of justice.
She is one of the most skilled soldiers in the series, renowned for her proficiency in using the Omni-Directional Mobility Gear and her combat abilities.

9.Frieren

Frieren is a character from the manga and anime series Frieren: Beyond Journey's End (Sōsō no Frieren), created by Kanehito Yamada and illustrated by Tsukasa Abe. The series explores themes of time, legacy, and the emotional impact of a long-lived being reflecting on a completed journey.
Frieren has long, silver hair, which she often wears loose.
She has light blue eyes.
She typically wears a mage's robe or similar attire that reflects her status as a powerful magician.
Frieren is a stoic and introspective character. She is a mage who has lived for centuries and has a detached demeanor, largely due to her long lifespan and the many changes she has witnessed.
Over time, she becomes more engaged with the people and the world around her, seeking to understand human emotions and experiences more deeply.
Frieren was part of a legendary hero party that defeated the Demon King. The story picks up after the end of their journey, focusing on Frieren's life as she reflects on her past adventures and seeks to understand the human experiences she has missed.
Her journey involves reconnecting with her past companions, dealing with the passage of time, and fulfilling promises made during her earlier adventures.
The series follows Frieren as she embarks on a new journey after her previous hero party's quest has concluded. She explores themes of memory, legacy, and the value of time, offering a unique perspective on the typical fantasy adventure narrative.
